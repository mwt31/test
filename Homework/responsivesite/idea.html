WHY

This website was built for the purpose of prototyping the Ça Va Vertica website. I built this website for my CIM 111 class however it was made in effort to hone in on key characteristics of Ça Va Vertica and how they could be displayed. The first page, is the home navigation and depicts a series of photogrpahs from the graphic t-shirt collection. As you scroll through the website you may have noticed the FLOW collection page, this page is dedicated to an upcoming first ever collection based on intricate handmade patchwork. If you go to the next page this is the gallery, as you can see, this page allows the audience to click on the photo and display a responsive aspect to the site. The intent of the gallery was to draw focus on the key photographs we have taken, all though there are many more, those are a couple of our favorites.
